Explore Mars!


You're about to send robots on an expedition to Mars.
You must write the instructions of the expedition in the textbox below.
Here's an example of the input format. You can copy it and paste it into the text box and send your first expedition now!

5 3
1 1 E
RFRFRFRF
3 2 N
FRRFLLFFRRFLL
0 3 W
LLFFFRFLFL



The first line of the instructions represents the x and y coordenates of the map of Mars.
Then you must write two lines for each robot:
The first one represents the starting position of the robot. That is the x and y coordinates as well as the orientation (N, E, S or W).
The second one represents the instructions the robot will follow (R, L or F).
Once you've send the robots to Mars, refresh the page and go to "Previous Expeditions" to check the journey's data.


When writing the instructions please keep in mind a few things:
- The maximum value for any coordenate must be 50
- The number of instructions you can assign to a robot must be less than 100
- The starting position of all robots must be within the map grid
